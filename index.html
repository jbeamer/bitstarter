This summer, we raised $1,000 for charity selling lemonade and cookies. That's pretty good, but we can do better! With 5 kids in the house, we always have lots of art -- our parents even throw some of it out! So here's the deal. You donate money right here on this page. We send you a piece of original art. It's awesome, so you share it with your friends, and they donate too! To keep costs low (after all, we want most of the money to go to our charities), we will usually scan in our creation and send it to you electronically. But, if you donate big enough, we will send you something!

FAQ

What will I get?
It depends on your donation. One of the kids will make you an original piece of art. Usually, this will be on paper, and we prefer to scan it in and send it to you electronically. For large donations, we may send a framed piece of art or a sculpture of some sort, but never if the cost to get it to you prevents us from meeting our goal of donating 90% to our charities.
How much goes to charity?
We will donate 90% of the proceeds to charity. The rest will be used for our art supplies, site hosting, and shipping our art to our larger donors. Our parents will subsidize our costs to ensure we hit the 90%
